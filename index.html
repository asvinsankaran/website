Projects & Roles

Captain – UBC Sailbot

I am currently the Captain of UBC Sailbot, an engineering design team of ~70 undergraduate students focussing on the design, construction, and testing of fully autonomous sailboats. Our previous project (pictured) was an 18ft sailboat we developed from scratch. Currently, we are in the design phase of a new project, capable of using this robotics technology to collect oceanic and atmospheric data in the Pacific Ocean. 

For the past 3+ years, I've always spent more time working on Sailbot when I should have been studying for classes. This team provides a unique opportunity to lead some highly innovative challenges and very passionate students. And where I started off learning a lot about technical engineering, I now spend my time teaching younger students while focussing more on high-level project management and technical integration.
