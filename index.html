I am currently the Captain of UBC Sailbot, an engineering design team of ~70 undergraduate students focusing on the design, construction, and testing of fully autonomous sailboats. Our previous project (pictured) was an 18ft sailboat we developed from scratch. Currently, we are in the design phase of a new project, capable of using this robotics technology to collect oceanic and atmospheric data in the Pacific Ocean.

For the past 3+ years, I've always spent more time working on Sailbot than studying. The team provides a unique opportunity to lead innovative challenges and passionate students. I started with technical engineering, and now I focus more on high-level project management and integration.
