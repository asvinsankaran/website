Fusion Reactor Design and Testing - Berlinguette Research Group UBC

The Berlinguette Group ›

In an ambitious 1 year capstone, we aimed to fuse ions together using plasma, a high voltage source, and complex nuclaear physics. In this project, we:

  • Designed, manufactured, and tested a large -30kV pulsing source to ignite the nuclear target
  • Manufactured a safety chamber for housing the plasma reactor
  • Iteratively tested the experiement by analyzing data to debug hardware issues
  • Created an RPI controls and data logging system for managing the experiment
